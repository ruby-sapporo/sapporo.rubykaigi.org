Title

Ruby on Rails: The Bad Parts

Abstract

Rails is getting more sophisticated as time goes by. However, there are many pitfalls out there. That is to say, Helper is useless, the layer we need doesn't exist fills between filter/partial and Engines and the misguiding concept like "Skinny Controller, Fat Model". In this talk, I will review these issues and give you some perspective in future developing of Rails applications.
