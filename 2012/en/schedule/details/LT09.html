Title

Rails Hackathon in Okinawa

Abstract

November 17th and 23-24th, a Rails hackathon will be held in Okinawa, a southern island in Japan. 17th is the day for ideathon in each local community and 23-24th are the days for getting together in Okinawa and show your product each other.

The hackathon is hosted by Minami.rb, Okinawa.rb, and Shibuya.rb. And we are planning a beach party after the hackathon! So, come on and join in Rails Hackathon in Okinawa!
